# Patna Business Analytics & Predictive Analytics

## Project Overview

This project analyzes local businesses in Patna using Python, Machine Learning, and Microsoft Power BI. The objective is to identify business trends, evaluate customer engagement, and predict digital growth opportunities.

## Dataset

The dataset contains 49 businesses with the following attributes:

- Business Name
- Category
- Address
- Phone Number
- Google Rating
- Total Reviews
- Price Level
- Working Hours
- Website Availability
- Digital Growth Opportunity

## Technologies Used

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Scikit-learn
- Microsoft Power BI
- DAX

## Machine Learning

- Random Forest Classifier
- Prediction of Digital Growth Opportunity

## Power BI Dashboard

The dashboard consists of three pages:

1. Executive Dashboard
2. Business Insights
3. Predictive Analytics & Recommendations

## Key Insights

- Businesses with higher ratings generally receive more customer reviews.
- Many businesses lack a website despite strong customer engagement.
- Working hours and business category significantly influence predicted digital growth.
- Restaurants and cafés represent the largest share of businesses in the dataset.
